1. One-month Pottery and Ceramic Introduction Course
Exclusive for beginners
  • Six sessions in one month
  • Three sessions per week, each lasting one hour
  • participants complete a minimum of five pieces of work during the 12-session course
  • Materials and tools are provided
  • Firing charges are based on the produced work

Course is a comprehensive exploration of pottery wheel and hand-building techniques. The course caters to individuals of all skill levels, whether seeking a creative outlet, exploring pottery for the first time, or aiming to deepen their understanding of clay as a sculptural material.Upon completing the introductory course and practicing for about six months, participants can progress to advanced courses. These courses offer an in-depth exploration of various techniques, allowing individuals to reach the level of expertise they desire. The duration of the advanced course is flexible, tailored to the depth of knowledge each participant wishes to achieve. This structured learning approach ensures a consistent and immersive experience, preparing individuals for an enriching journey in the world of pottery.

2.Private Pottery Session
Crafted to provide a personalized and memorable experience
  • One-hour clay sessions are designed for individuals
  • All materials and tools provided
  • Session fee of ₹1000 per person ensures an individualized experience
  • Discounted rates of ₹900 each for groups of at least 4 participants and ₹800 each for larger groups of 9 or more
  • Requiring a confirmation charge of ₹1000.
  • 3 sessions in a day
  • 3 pm - 4 pm
  • 6 pm - 7 pm
  • 7 pm - 8 pm
  • Wednesday Closed
  • Sunday 11 am - 6 pm

These one-hour clay sessions are designed for individuals who wish to spend an hour engaging in the creative process and leave with a beautiful, handcrafted pottery piece. Each session covers hand-built techniques, pinching, and personalization, ensuring that you not only enjoy the process but also create a unique clay masterpiece. Advance booking is essential, requiring a confirmation charge of ₹1000. Join us for an hour of creative expression, where you can delve into the world of pottery, craft something beautiful, and make your artistic vision come to life.
